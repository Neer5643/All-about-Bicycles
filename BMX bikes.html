What are BMX Bicycles?
They were knockoffs of motocross motorcycles and were designed for racing over jumps and around berms in the dirt. ... BMX bikes are still designed for racing, although your child doesn't have to race to enjoy the light weight, speed and dirt-worthiness of these machines. They have 20-inch wheels, 2- or 4-piece handlebars, small seats, long cranks and strong rear hand brakes. The frames are light and sturdy, and the higher the price, the lighter they get.
